<p><p>Vapor-liquid entrainment separators are specialized equipment used to separate vapor and liquid phases in industrial processes. The market typically consists of horizontal and vertical entrainment separators. Horizontal separators are designed to effectively manage large volumes of liquid and vapor, utilizing gravity to enhance separation efficiency. In contrast, vertical separators minimize footprint and are suitable for systems with limited space, effectively using centrifugal forces to aid in the separation process. Both types play critical roles in optimizing efficiency and product purity in various applications.</p></p>
