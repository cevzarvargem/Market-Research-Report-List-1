<p><ul><li>Single Mode Cable</li><li>Multimode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speaker cable market can be categorized into two main types: single-mode and multimode cables. Single-mode cables are designed for high-frequency signals and longer distances, typically used in high-performance audio systems, providing clearer sound with minimal loss. In contrast, multimode cables are better suited for shorter distances and are often more cost-effective, making them ideal for standard home audio setups. Both types cater to different user needs depending on the audio quality and range requirements of the system.</p></p>
