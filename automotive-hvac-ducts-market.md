<p><p>The automotive HVAC ducts market is categorized into gas ducts and liquid ducts. Gas ducts primarily facilitate the distribution of heated or cooled air throughout the vehicle's cabin, ensuring optimal passenger comfort. These ducts are designed for airflow efficiency and may be integrated with various materials to enhance performance. Liquid ducts, on the other hand, are used for transporting coolant to regulate temperature in the engine and cabin systems. Both types are essential for effective climate control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
