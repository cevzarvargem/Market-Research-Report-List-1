<p><ul><li>Synthetic</li><li>Biological</li><li>Biosynthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endovascular stent grafts market comprises three main types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. </p><p>Synthetic stent grafts are primarily made from materials like polyester or polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), offering durability and biocompatibility for vascular repairs. </p><p>Biological stent grafts utilize human or animal tissues, promoting natural healing and integration within the body but have limitations in availability and shelf life. </p><p>Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic materials with biological elements, aiming to enhance compatibility and tissue integration, thereby bridging the gap between the other two types.</p></p>
