<p><ul><li>Acquisition System</li><li>Production System</li><li>Release System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Map Ecosystem comprises three key market types: Acquisition System, Production System, and Release System. Acquisition Systems focus on data collection, utilizing various technologies like satellite imagery and sensors to gather geographic information. Production Systems involve processing and refining this data into usable formats, ensuring accuracy and relevance. Release Systems manage the distribution and delivery of the final digital maps to users, including web and mobile platforms, ensuring accessibility and integration with other applications and services.</p></p>
