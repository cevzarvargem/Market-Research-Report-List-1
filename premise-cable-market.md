<p><ul><li>Copper Cable</li><li>Fiber Optic cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The premise cable market consists primarily of two types: copper cables and fiber optic cables. Copper cables, traditionally used for telecommunications and networking, are known for their electrical conductivity and reliability but have limitations in bandwidth and distance. In contrast, fiber optic cables utilize light signals for data transmission, offering superior speed, higher bandwidth, and longer distances without signal loss. As demand for high-speed internet and advanced communication systems increases, the market for fiber optics is rapidly growing, while copper remains relevant for certain applications.</p></p>
