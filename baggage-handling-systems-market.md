<p><p>The baggage handling systems market consists of several types, including RFID, barcode, and AI systems. RFID baggage handling systems use radio-frequency identification to track and manage luggage seamlessly, enhancing tracking accuracy and reducing loss. Barcode systems rely on printed labels scanned at various checkpoints, offering a cost-effective solution for tracking. AI baggage handling systems leverage artificial intelligence to optimize operations, predict baggage flow, and improve efficiency. Together, these technologies enhance the overall effectiveness of airport luggage management and customer experience.</p></p>
