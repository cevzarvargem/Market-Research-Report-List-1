<p><p>The Torque Wrench Pump market can be categorized into three main types: manual driven, air driven, and electric driven. Air driven pumps use compressed air for efficient torque application, making them suitable for medium to high-volume tasks. Electric driven pumps offer the greatest efficiency and speed, automating the torque application process for large-scale operations, enhancing accuracy and reducing operator fatigue in various industrial settings.</p></p>
