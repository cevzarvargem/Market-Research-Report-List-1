<p><p>The Styrenics Resin market is primarily categorized into four types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), Styrene-Acrylonitrile (SAN), and Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS). GPPS is valued for its clarity and rigidity. HIPS offers enhanced impact resistance suitable for various applications. SAN blends durability with transparency, making it ideal for high-performance uses. ABS combines toughness and flexibility, widely used in automotive and consumer goods, highlighting the versatility and diverse applications of styrenic resins across industries.</p></p>
