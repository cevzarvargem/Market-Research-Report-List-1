<p><strong>The Biped Walking Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Walk</li><li>Quasi-dynamic Walk</li><li>Not dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biped walking robots can be classified into three market types based on their walking capabilities. Static walking robots maintain balance through a fixed center of gravity, making them stable but often slow. Quasi-dynamic walking robots combine elements of static and dynamic walking, allowing for more fluid movement while still retaining some stability. Dynamic walking robots, on the other hand, utilize active motion and gait adaptation, enabling faster and more agile movements. Each type caters to different applications and performance requirements in robotics.</p></p>
