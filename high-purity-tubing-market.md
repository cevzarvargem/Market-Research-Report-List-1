<p><p>The High Purity Tubing market is primarily categorized into two types: Stainless Steel and Fluoropolymer. Stainless Steel tubing is known for its durability, resistance to high temperatures, and compatibility with various fluids, making it ideal for pharmaceutical and chemical applications. Fluoropolymer tubing, on the other hand, offers exceptional chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it suitable for applications involving corrosive substances. Both types ensure minimal contamination, maintaining the integrity of high-purity processes in critical industries.</p></p>
