<p><p>The epitaxial equipment market encompasses various technologies used to deposit thin films on substrates for semiconductor manufacturing. Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) equipment is primarily used for III-V compound semiconductors, critical in optoelectronics. Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) equipment specializes in ultra-high vacuum environments to achieve high-quality crystalline layers, often for research applications. Other CVD devices include variations of chemical vapor deposition methods that cater to specific materials or processes, contributing to diverse applications in electronics and photonics.</p></p>
