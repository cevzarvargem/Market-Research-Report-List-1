<p><p>The Reusable Satellite Launch Vehicle (RSLV) market consists of two main types: partially reusable and fully reusable systems. Partially reusable vehicles utilize components, such as boosters or stages, that can be refurbished and reused for multiple launches, reducing costs and resource waste. In contrast, fully reusable vehicles are designed to return to Earth completely intact for refurbishment, allowing for multiple consecutive launches with significant cost savings and environmental benefits. Both market types aim to enhance the affordability and efficiency of space access.</p></p>
