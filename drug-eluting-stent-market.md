These specialized stents are coated with drugs that elute over time, significantly reducing the likelihood of complications associated with conventional bare-metal stents.</p><p>The Drug-Eluting Stent Market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by an increase in cardiovascular diseases, advancements in stent technology, and a growing aging population. As these trends evolve, the DES market is poised for significant transformation, catering to the growing needs of patients and healthcare providers alike.</p></p>
