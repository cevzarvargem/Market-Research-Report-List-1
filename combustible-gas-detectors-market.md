<p><strong>The Combustible Gas Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conductivity Combustible Gas Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Combustible Gas Detectors</li><li>Catalytic Combustible Gas Detectors</li><li>Infrared Optical Combustible Gas Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The combustible gas detectors market comprises several key types. Thermal conductivity detectors measure gas concentration by assessing thermal properties. Semiconductor detectors utilize materials whose resistance changes in the presence of specific gases. Catalytic detectors rely on oxidation reactions on a heated element to detect flammable gases. Infrared optical detectors identify gas concentration through infrared absorption techniques, offering high sensitivity. Each type caters to different applications, ensuring safety and compliance in industrial and residential settings.</p></p>
