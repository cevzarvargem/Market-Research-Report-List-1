<p><p>The oil-free vacuum pumps market includes various types, each serving specific applications. Screw vacuum pumps utilize rotating helical screws to create a vacuum, offering high efficiency and reliability. Rotary vane vacuum pumps use sliding vanes to create suction and are ideal for moderate vacuum levels. Piston vacuum pumps generate vacuum through reciprocating pistons and are effective for high vacuum applications. Membrane vacuum pumps employ flexible membranes to create a vacuum without oil contamination, catering to sensitive processes. Other types encompass a variety of specialized designs.</p></p>
