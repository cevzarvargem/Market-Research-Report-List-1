<p><ul><li>Dye Type</li><li>Pigment Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printer ink market primarily consists of two types: dye-based and pigment-based inks. Dye-type inks are known for their vibrant colors and are typically used in photo printers for high-quality images. However, they are less resistant to UV light and water, making them less durable. In contrast, pigment-type inks offer better longevity and resistance to fading, making them suitable for professional and archival printing. Each type serves different consumer needs, influencing their applications in various industries.</p></p>
