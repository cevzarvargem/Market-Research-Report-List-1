<p><p>The powered support market includes three main types: chock support, shield support, and chock shield support. Chock support refers to individual supports that provide stability in mining operations, allowing for flexible positioning. Shield support involves larger, continuous structures that protect miners while enabling equipment movement. Chock shield support combines features of both, offering structural support and worker protection with enhanced mobility. Each type is designed to improve safety, increase efficiency, and adapt to varying mining conditions and configurations.</p></p>
