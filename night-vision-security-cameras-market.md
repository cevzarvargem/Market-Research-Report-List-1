In contrast, PTZ cameras offer flexibility, allowing users to remotely adjust the camera's position, zoom in on subjects, and cover a broader area. This adaptability makes PTZ cameras suitable for varied environments, enhancing surveillance capabilities during low-light conditions.</p></p>
