<p><ul><li>Dynamic Orthotics</li><li>Static Orthotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ankle orthoses market consists of dynamic and static orthotics, each serving distinct purposes. Dynamic orthotics are designed to allow some movement while providing support, aiding in rehabilitation by promoting mobility and facilitating muscle function. In contrast, static orthotics offer rigid support to immobilize the ankle, commonly used for injury prevention or post-surgery recovery. These two types address varying patient needs, ensuring tailored treatment options for conditions such as sprains, fractures, and chronic instability.</p></p>
