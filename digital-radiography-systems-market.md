<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Radiography systems are categorized into two main types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR technology utilizes traditional X-ray equipment but employs a digital phosphor plate for image capture, which is then processed and converted into digital images. In contrast, DR technology involves a direct digital acquisition process, allowing for immediate image viewing through flat-panel detectors. Both systems enhance diagnostic capabilities, improve workflow efficiency, and reduce patient exposure to radiation, thus playing a vital role in modern medical imaging.</p></p>
