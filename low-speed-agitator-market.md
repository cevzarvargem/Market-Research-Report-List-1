<p><strong>The Low Speed Agitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anchor Impeller</li><li>Helical Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-speed agitator market primarily features two types of impellers: anchor impellers and helical impellers. Anchor impellers are designed for high-viscosity fluids, providing effective mixing by scraping vessel walls and encouraging flow. They are ideal for applications in adhesives and paints. In contrast, helical impellers facilitate efficient fluid motion with a design that promotes axial flow, making them suitable for medium to low-viscosity materials. Both types are essential for industries like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals, ensuring optimal blending and mixing performance.</p></p>
