<p><p>The refrigerants market comprises three main types: fluorocarbons, inorganics, and hydrocarbons. Fluorocarbons, including HFCs, are synthetic compounds widely used for their effective thermal properties but face regulatory challenges due to environmental impacts. Inorganics, such as ammonia and water, are eco-friendly alternatives with high efficiency but require careful handling due to toxicity. Hydrocarbons, like propane and isobutane, offer low environmental impact and excellent thermal performance, making them increasingly popular in refrigeration applications while also presenting flammability concerns.</p></p>
