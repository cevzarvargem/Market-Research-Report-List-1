<p><strong>The Ballistic Security Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Laminated Glass</li><li>Insulated Ballistic Glass</li><li>Acrylic</li><li>Polycarbonate</li><li>Glass-clad Polycarbonate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ballistic security glass market includes various types designed for enhanced protection. Traditional laminated glass comprises two sheets of glass with an interlayer, offering basic resistance to impacts. Insulated ballistic glass combines laminated layers with insulating properties, improving energy efficiency. Acrylic, a lightweight alternative, offers decent ballistic resistance but can scratch easily. Polycarbonate is a robust, impact-resistant material favored for its strength and lightweight nature. Glass-clad polycarbonate integrates a glass exterior with a polycarbonate core, providing superior protection while maintaining aesthetics.</p></p>
