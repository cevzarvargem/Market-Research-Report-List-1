<p><p>The industrial carbohydrase market comprises three primary enzyme types: amylase, glucanase, and cellulase. Amylase breaks down starch into sugars, playing a crucial role in food, brewing, and biofuel industries. Glucanase targets beta-glucans, aiding in the processing of grains and improving animal feed digestibility. Cellulase decomposes cellulose, facilitating waste treatment and biofuel production from plant materials. These enzymes are essential for enhancing efficiency and sustainability in various industrial processes, contributing to high demand across multiple sectors.</p></p>
