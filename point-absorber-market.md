<p><p>A point absorber is a type of wave energy converter that captures and converts the energy produced by surface waves into usable electricity. Typically, these devices consist of a buoy that floats on the water surface and a submerged component that moves in response to wave motion. This movement drives generators that produce power. The versatility and efficiency of point absorbers make them increasingly popular in the renewable energy sector.</p><p>The Point Absorber Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period. As governments and organizations aim to meet renewable energy targets, investments in ocean energy technologies are rising. Furthermore, the growing awareness of climate change and the need for reduction in carbon emissions are propelling more research and development in this field. Recent trends also show an emphasis on hybrid systems that combine point absorbers with other renewable technologies, aiming for a more integrated approach to energy generation from marine resources.</p></p>
